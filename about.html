WHO WE ARE

More than wall art — AmiLuna is a visual experience.

AmiLuna is a digital canvas art brand focused on creating artwork that brings elegance, atmosphere, and emotional depth into modern spaces. Every piece begins as digitally crafted art and is refined into a canvas experience designed to make rooms feel intentional, expressive, and alive.

Rather than simply filling a wall, AmiLuna aims to shape the mood of a space. The collection is built around softness, contrast, visual warmth, and carefully composed forms that allow each artwork to feel both modern and timeless.

UNDER THE UMBRELLA OF

AZG Group

AmiLuna operates under AZG Group, a creative and digital innovation company built around design, technology, storytelling, and independent brand creation.

As part of AZG Group, AmiLuna reflects a broader vision of building meaningful digital products and artistic experiences that combine creativity with modern execution.

FOUNDER

Aisha Z

AmiLuna was founded by Aisha Hamid Mohamed(Aisha Z), the creator behind AZG Group. Built from an independent vision, AmiLuna represents the intersection of art, design, technology, and storytelling.

Through AmiLuna, the goal is not only to create beautiful artwork, but to build a brand that offers identity, atmosphere, and visual meaning for every space it enters.
